# 📊 Executive MIS Reporting & Analytics Platform

An Executive MIS Reporting & Analytics Platform developed during my internship to centralize operational reporting, automate reporting workflows, and provide management with real-time business insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## 📖 Overview

This repository showcases the Executive MIS Reporting & Analytics Platform that I designed and developed during my internship. The solution consolidated multiple disconnected operational reports into a centralized reporting platform, enabling real-time monitoring of key business operations through interactive dashboards.

The platform streamlines reporting across multiple business functions by combining Google Sheets, Google Apps Script, and Looker Studio into an automated reporting workflow that supports daily operational monitoring and management decision-making.
